My Top 4 Favorite Things To Do In Maui

Home Page


Haleakala National Park

  • One of the most breathtaking national parks in the world
  • This national park reaches above 10,000 ft in elevation
  • I recommend coming here for sunrise or sunset for a beautiful view over the island of Maui

Road to Hana

  • The Road to Hana is one of the most scenic drives in all of Hawaii!
  • This drive has such gorgeous views of the coast and sometimes you can see whales.
  • Make sure you check out the black sand beaches as well as the local banana bread markets.

Molokini Crater

  • This rock formation forms a crater in the middle of the ocean right next to Maui.
  • This reef is home to over 200 different species of Fish.
  • If you are lucky enough, you may run into a reef tip shark.
Waimoku Falls
  • This Waterfall is over 200 feet tall
  • If you're feeling brave enough, don't be afraid to hop in the water in swim in this beautiful waterfall.
  • This waterfall is in the heart of Maui's beloved rainforests.
